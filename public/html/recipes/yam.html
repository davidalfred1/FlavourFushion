Yam Porridge

Yam porridge is a hearty and comforting dish made by simmering yam cubes in a flavorful broth with spices, vegetables, and sometimes meat or fish. Yam porridge is a popular West African dish, especially in Nigeria and Ghana, where yams are a staple crop. Yam porridge is rich in carbohydrates, fiber, and vitamins, making it a satisfying and nutritious meal option.
