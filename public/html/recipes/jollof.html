Jollof

Jollof rice with spicy chicken is a flavorful West African dish consisting of seasoned rice cooked with tomatoes, onions, peppers, and spices, typically served with grilled or fried chicken marinated in a spicy blend of herbs and spices. Jollof rice is a beloved dish across West Africa and is often served at celebrations, gatherings, and special occasions. Jollof rice is rich in carbohydrates and provides a good source of protein from the chicken. It also contains various vitamins and minerals from the vegetables and spices used in its preparation.
